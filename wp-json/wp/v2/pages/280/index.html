EKVA is a digital platform that aids waste collectors and EPR companies to identify waste distribution all the while gamifying the process of waste disposal for users. Utilizing gamification tactics, <\/span>EKVA motivates and educates users about the importance of properly disposing and sorting waste. The data leveraged from the <\/span>EKVA system allows companies to visualize impactful patterns\/insights, streamline costs and calculate and mitigate risks. The system consists of a mobile app for users paired with a dashboard that provides key KPI\u2019s including consumer disposal patterns and waste distribution that are essential for EPR companies and waste collectors.
